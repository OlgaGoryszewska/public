Frequently Asked Questions

What materials are your rain jackets made of?

Our rain jackets are made from a variety of materials, including waterproof and breathable fabrics like Gore-Tex and eVent. We also use recycled and eco-friendly materials whenever possible.

How do I choose the right size for my rain jacket?

We offer sizing charts on each product page to help you choose the right size. If you're still unsure, please don't hesitate to contact us for personalized sizing advice.

How do I care for my rain jacket?

We recommend following the care instructions on the tag of your rain jacket, as different materials may require different care. In general, it's best to wash your rain jacket with mild detergent in cold water, and avoid using fabric softeners or bleach. Tumble dry on low heat or hang to dry.

Do you offer any warranties or guarantees?

We stand behind the quality of our products and offer a one-year warranty against manufacturing defects. If you experience any issues with your rain jacket, please contact us and we'll be happy to help you with a repair, replacement, or refund.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we offer international shipping to many countries. Please check our shipping page for more information on international shipping rates and delivery times.

Can I return or exchange my rain jacket?

We offer a 30-day return and exchange policy on all unused and unwashed products. If you're not satisfied with your rain jacket, please contact us and we'll be happy to help you with a return or exchange.
